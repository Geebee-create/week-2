More About Me

My Interest in Coding

I want to become a front-end software developer as it will allow me to blend my creativity with problem-solving. Coding is like crafting; it's a way to transform ideas into something tangible and functional. My diverse life experiences, including my background in English studies, provide me with a unique perspective and strong communication skills; I plan to apply these to software development, as they have enhanced my ability to understand user needs. During my formative years, the notion of pursuing a career in software development seemed unattainable as British comprehensive schools lacked coding education. However, participating in a rigorous software development boot camp has shown me that I possess the essential skills needed for this field. Learning to code opens a world of possibilities, and I want to contribute to the creative force driving technological advancements.

My Background and Education

My previous education includes English and Drama A levels, a 2.1 in English BA (Hons) and an MA in Fashion Buying and Merchandising Management. My work experience is varied and includes retail, bar, office, estate agent and teaching assistant work. I also have a PGCE in Primary Education from the University of Manchester. While I chose not to continue pursuing a career in primary education, it was an extremely challenging course that included:

  • Multitasking
  • The writing of academic papers on educational psychology
  • Preparing and teaching full weeks of lessons for 30+ children
  • Completing vast amounts of paperwork and marking

I am grateful for the experience as it strengthened my stamina and taught me that ultimately, I want to work in a business centered, creative role. I was also able to experience the teaching of basic coding skills to young children. This opened my eyes to the importance of coding in the modern world, and coding education is something that I believe should have been introduced in the primary setting decades earlier.

Doing an MA in Fashion Buying and Merchandising Management allowed me to explore my interest in sales, E-Commerce and sustainability. It improved my tech skills as I was taught how to use Google Analytics, Excel, Adobe Illustrate, InDesign and Photoshop. It also enhanced my understanding of the business world and my analytical and competitive analysis skills. During my MA, I created the video (shown below) using Canva. I received a distinction grade of 78 for this assignment and enjoyed the creative process.
